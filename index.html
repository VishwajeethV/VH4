Mission

VH4 Aerospace exists to make suborbital spaceflight affordable through reusable hybrid rocket technology and efficient manufacturing. Rather than optimizing purely for peak performance, Prometheus-1 is engineered around affordability, manufacturability, rapid refurbishment, and operational simplicity.
